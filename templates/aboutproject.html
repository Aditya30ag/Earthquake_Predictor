Project Overview

This health risk assessment tool uses machine learning to predict potential health risks based on various parameters.

How It Works

The system takes three key parameters as input and processes them through a trained machine learning model to assess potential health risks.

Risk Levels

  • No Risk (< 4)
  • Low Risk (4-6)
  • Moderate Risk (6-8)
  • High Risk (8-9)
  • Very High Risk (> 9)
